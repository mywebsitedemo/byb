By addressing the root cause, rather than the symptoms, Systematic Kinesiology, combined with conventional functional testing when necessary, enables me to identify the complexity of each of my client’s ailments.

One condition has many different causes and, likewise, one cause may result in many different conditions. As a result, the aim is to create a programme to address the specific manifestations of disease in each individual.

If our cells have inadequate nutrients, they work less efficiently and our health becomes compromised. Our physical and emotional health is affected by our eating habits, inadequate sleep, by high levels stress, toxins and anti nutrients to which we are exposed to through our air, water and food.

Kinesiology is a complementary health therapy developed by Chiropractor Dr George Goodheart in 1964, in which he created simple muscle testing techniques to identify imbalances within the body systems.

Once imbalances have been identified a variety of techniques may be used which include:

  • Holding neurovascular points on the head to release stress and tension
  • Massaging neuro lymphatic points on the body
  • Running acupuncture meridians
  • Recommending nutritional support
  • Recommending positive lifestyle changes
