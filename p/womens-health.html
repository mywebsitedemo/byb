“My hormones feel so out of balance” a female will tell me. “I’m tired of feeling tired, tearful, bloated and edgy much the time. “Is there a natural way to get my hormones in balance?”

Hormone imbalances are epidemic these days. When talking about hormones it’s important to understand that all of them interact, so when one gets out of sync, others quickly follow. There are many key players including adrenals, thyroid, insulin – but importantly women’s sex hormones can impact not only on a women’s physical health but emotional too.

While many things can cause an imbalance in our hormones, the good news is that many women can fix these imbalances without the use of medication.

Women may struggle from mood swings resulting from the three P’s or the three M’s:

  • Puberty
  • premenstrual syndrome (PMS)
  • peri-menopause
  • menstrual cramps
  • menopause
  • mental anxiety!

    • Did you know that a woman’s hormone levels can drop up to 90 percent during the aging process? Are women destined to suffer from impaired mood, muscle loss, poor sleep, memory difficulties, and sexual problems? Of course not!

    The struggles related to our reproductive life cycle are unnecessary. It is not a result of bad luck; it’s due to bad habits, such as drinking alcohol and smoking; eating a high-sugar and refined-carbohydrate diet; consuming dairy and often, gluten; not exercising enough; being exposed to environmental toxins; and being chronically stressed.

    Simply put, PMS, menopausal symptoms, and other problems are all signs of imbalances in your sex hormones. They are not the result of poor genes that destroy our sexual vitality as we age. Instead, they are treatable symptoms of underlying imbalance in one of the core systems in your body.

    By identifying what is creating the imbalances – we will address the underlying problem, restore balance, and the symptoms will diminish.
