There are experiences and situations in life that may cause us stress, anxiety, fatigue and negative thoughts. Sometimes it's difficult to know how to move forward and we may just feel stuck.

My intention is to create a safe space in which you can explore a process for addressing specific issues of concern, and to look at limiting beliefs which could be affecting your ability to make decisions and achieve your goals.

By exploring the way you think, and altering this in a positive way, will not only assist you in creating the reality that you desire, but also to re connect to the joy of living, feel centred and have a greater sense awareness. This will be an opportunity to gradually rediscover the real you.

I believe that positive change takes place when you feel in control and are happy about the choices you are making in life.

    If your aim is to:

    Identify ways to change your life for the better. Stick to a commitment you have made to improve your life. Improve your general health both physically and emotionally. Overcome the effects of earlier poor health.

    Effective coaching can help you:

    Allow you the space and support to keep you on track. Clarify your motivation for changing your life in positive ways. Feel positive about taking on the challenge. Understand why past attempts may have been unsuccessful. Work out a realistic strategy and timeline.

    Coaching enables you to understand yourself better and thereby reach a level of self-acceptance that will enable you to make informed choices based on who you really are. The removal of limiting beliefs and discarding unhelpful emotions are some of the major keys to happiness. It is not about trying to change you, it's about exploring the capacity to view things differently and possibly reacting to situations and people in a different way in order to achieve a more positive outcome.
